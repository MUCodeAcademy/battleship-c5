Welcome to Battleship!

Place 4 ships of different sizes vertically or horizontally on your board. You will then take turns trying to guess where your opponents enemy ships are located. When all of a player's ships have been sunk, the opposing player wins.

  • Destroyer - 2x1 ship
  • Submarine - 3x1 ship
  • Battleship - 4x1
  • Carrier - 5x1 ship
